    Who we are

    Our main product base is Cashew nuts and everything cashew - and there is a lot with the fruit than just the apple and the nuts. For the past 17 years, we've supplied local processors who unshelled the nut for international market.

    The Cashew Juice Honey Molasé is our first manufactured product from Kwara State. We have other products pending NAFDAC approval. Hoping to introduce the freshly squeezed Cashew Juice early next year. The Cashew Wine and Brandy will follow thereafter under our registered Trademark. Our super big Banana plantain are likely ready for market later this year or early next year.

    These are long time ideas that are just now manifesting after some summersult due to the peculiar business environment. Plans are in place to continue to create employment for our massive youths who are ready to work. 90% of our team are bright fellows. And we thank you for your continued support.

    St. Jude Farms & Produce NIg Ltd is a subsidiary of St. Jude Investment Company Ltd.

    Our Value

    Cashews are cash crops, harvested mainly for its nut, the so-called cashew apple, is usually thrown away or left to rot as a by-product of the harvest. Statistics also shows that Africa produces 57 percent of the world’s favorite kidney-shaped snack, but 90 percent of the crop is exported raw and processed elsewhere, creating jobs and wealth that should be staying in Africa.

    Here at St. Jude Farms, we are changing this narrative, We beleive there’s alot of opportunity from agricultural waste. Oppotunity to create jobs for the teeaming youth, the Oppotunity to turn waste to wealth. This is at the core of what we do at St. Jude Farms, that is what drives us continually, as we put seed in the ground to the time for harvest.
